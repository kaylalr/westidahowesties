Puppies

Daisy

Daisy definitely likes to do her own thing. When we take her on walks, she likes to wonder off. And when we call her name, she takes her time coming back to us. She doesn't like to cuddle very much, unless she's pregnangt, and she likes her space. If one of the other dogs/puppies tries to take her treat or toy, she lets them know quickly that that's not going to happen.

Misty

Mistie is short for Mystic, but we only call her Mystic if she's in a lot of trouble. She is definitely the cudler of the group. Is cudler a word? She will come up to you wanting to just sit in your lap and sleep while you work on the computer. She likes barking at cars that drive by or people in our neighbors yard. But it's not an angry bark. She's just letting us know that someone is there, and when they get close, she is the first to go get attention from them.

Poppy

Poppy is like the youngest kid in the family. She knows that she can get away with a lot and uses it to her advantage. She is actually Daisy's daughter and we're still trying to train her not to chew everything is sight. She always want all the attention and feels left out if you are petting on of the other dogs and not her. But we still lover her. Well, most of us anyway.
